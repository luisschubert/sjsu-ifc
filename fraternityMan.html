I am a Fraternity Man

Here at San Jose State, the Interfraternity Council asked members of the campus community what comes to mind when they hear the word 'Fraternity'. In this video, people tell us the truth about what they think about fraternities.

"What does 'Fraternity' make you think of?" is the first video of SJSU's IAAFM series to bring into focus the vast majority of young men whose fraternity experiences are positive and nothing like the negative ones portrayed in the media.

San Jose State University's Interfraternity Council stands with the national I Am Fraternity Man movement in support to share the true stories of fraternity men that you didn't know existed.
